Places near UCL that serve Vegetarian Food


  1. Franco Manca

    Where: 98 Tottenham Court Rd, Bloomsbury, London W1T 4TR

    Forget cabbage soup, Enjoy delicous pizza at reasonable prices at franco manca


  2. Indian YMCA restaurant

    Where: 41 Fitzroy Street, London, W1T 6AQ

    The Indian YMCA houses a restaurant that is open to the general public, providing cheap and authentic Indian cuisine in the heart of Bloomsbury, close to UCL's main campus.

    Get to taste the flavours of india. Very affordable for UCL students. Vegetarian curry for £2 and Chicken Curry for £3.


  3. Wasabi

    Where: 350 Euston Rd, Fitzrovia, London NW1 3AX

    Offers fresh, authentic, handmade Japanese Sushi and Bento. A wide food range to pick from; fresh sushi, soup, Bento, teppan, salad.

    There is something for everyone at Wasabi; veg and non-veg items.

    Review: recommended dish is the classic Chicken Katsu Curry. Great value for money, ideal for a quick bite on the run.


  4. Benito's Hat

    Where: 56 Goodge St, Fitzrovia, London W1T 4NB

    Benito's Hat Menu: Ordered: Grilled vegetable naked burrito (£5.80, £5.10 with student discount) and braised chicken naked burrito (£6.80, £6.00 with student discount)

    Review: The service was really good the food itself was delicious and fresh. Would definitely recommend it!

    Benito's Hat adds to the popular burrito market, it is perhaps the most filling food for under £8 in London.

    Get the naked version with grilled chicken and you have a fairly healthy meal, but nothing beats the on-the-go practicality of a burrito.

    Very quick and efficient service!
